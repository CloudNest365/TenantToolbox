# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want an open, welcoming and harassment-free environment for everyone —
regardless of experience, background, identity or orientation.

## Expected behavior

- Communicate respectfully and constructively.
- Value differing viewpoints and experiences.
- Accept and give constructive criticism.
- Focus on what is best for the project and the community.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Insulting, demeaning or harassing comments.
- Personal or political attacks, trolling.
- Publishing others' private information without permission.

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to the project maintainers (CloudNest365). All
complaints are reviewed and handled confidentially. Maintainers may remove
comments, commits, issues and PRs, or ban contributors.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the
[Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org), version 2.1.
